Black Galaxy Granite

The World's Most Desired Granite

Black Galaxy Granite (also known as Star Galaxy) is quarried exclusively around Chimakurthy, Andhra Pradesh. Its unique gold-speckled appearance on a jet-black base makes it one of the most sought-after natural stones globally — perfect for luxury flooring, exterior cladding, kitchen countertops, and architectural features.

  • Exceptional hardness and durability
  • Mirror-finish polish available
  • Available in slabs, tiles & custom cuts
  • Suitable for flooring, cladding & countertops
  • Temperature- and scratch-resistant
  • Export-grade crating & packaging
